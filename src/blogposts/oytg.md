(inspired by https://notnite.com/blog/oytg)

this is a sort of non-exhaustive list of games I really like! i'm generally a huge fan of movement mechanics in games and im obsessed with ones that have a strong stylistic direction

## Adventure
- [DELTARUNE](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1671210/DELTARUNE/): the parallel story to UNDERTALE, it fascinates me in ways i cannot fully explain
- [ENA: Dream BBQ](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2134320/ENA_Dream_BBQ/): an incredibly artistic surreal interactive adventure. please play it 
- [Celeste](https://store.steampowered.com/app/504230/Celeste/): transgender videogame. amazing 2D platformer, manages to be difficult without being too punishing
- [A Hat in Time](https://store.steampowered.com/app/253230/A_Hat_in_Time/): the cutest 3D platformer, has great modding support too
- [Funi Raccoon Game](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3599690/Funi_Raccoon_Game/): little raccoon collect many trash across a bunch of silly worlds
- [The Stanley Parable: Ultra Deluxe](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1703340/The_Stanley_Parable_Ultra_Deluxe/): This is the story of a man named Stanley

## Puzzle
- [Helltaker](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1289310/Helltaker/) - a game about demon girls. great music
- [Awaria](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3274300/Awaria/) - a game about ghost girls. also has great music

## Other Indie
- [UNBEATABLE](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2240620/UNBEATABLE/): a stylish rhythm game with a story that made me cry for hours straight. please play it.
- [MINDWAVE](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2701030/MINDWAVE/): a sort of really creative Warioware-like with narrative elements and great music
- [ULTRAKILL](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1229490/ULTRAKILL/): THE stylish boomer shooter, has an insane amount of skill expression and intriguing lore
- [Neon White](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1533420/Neon_White/): a kind of FPS/Speedrunning/3D platformer hybrid, pretty fun! also the music is by Machine Girl
- [Schedule I](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3164500/Schedule_I/): a pretty good tycoon game, always turns into a week-long hyperfixation when i come back to it
- [Buckshot Roulette](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2835570/Buckshot_Roulette/) - russian roulette with a 12-gauge shotgun, has a really fun multiplayer mode too
- [Cruelty Squad](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1388770/Cruelty_Squad/): I've been getting really into "hell". Both as a mindset and as something to strive for, in an organizational sense.

## Action
- [METAL GEAR SOLID series](https://store.steampowered.com/franchise/METALGEAR): all of these aged like fine wine
- [Yakuza / Like a Dragon series](https://store.steampowered.com/franchise/RyuGaGotokuStudio/list/124766): putting autistic men in the stupidest situations possible is pretty funny
- [Judgment series](https://store.steampowered.com/franchise/RyuGaGotokuStudio/list/124769/), also by the Yakuza devs
- [Hi-Fi RUSH](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1817230/HiFi_RUSH/): a charming rhythm-based action game in which everything moves to the beat

## Multiplayer
- [FINAL FANTASY XIV ONLINE](https://na.finalfantasyxiv.com/): an MMORPG with a stupidly generous free trial, probably is my most played game by hour count
- [Deadlock](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1422450/Deadlock/): genuinely the most stylish multiplayer title from any of the big studios, and the movement mechanics are enough for me to not care that it's effectively a MOBA
- [Deep Rock Galactic](https://store.steampowered.com/app/548430/Deep_Rock_Galactic/): fun co-op digging and shooting game to dick around with friends in
- [Goblin Cleanup](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2748340/Goblin_Cleanup/): clean up and prepare dungeons with friends before the next adventurers come in
- [PEAK](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3527290/PEAK/): climb big mountain with friends. beware of monsters
- [R.E.P.O.](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3241660/REPO/): a silly friendslop game where you collect valuable items while trying not to get killed. i love the little robot guys you play as

## Miscellaneous
- [DEATH STRANDING](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1850570/DEATH_STRANDING_DIRECTORS_CUT/): having to balance a stupid amount of cargo on your back is actually pretty engaging. i find the story very touching considering kojima's history as well as the covid lockdown
- **Paper Mario: The Thousand Year Door**: probably the best out of all the paper mario titles
- **Sonic Riders**: PEAK RACING. very skill based and oozing with style. i miss it dearly.
- Anything by **Valve**, really. **Half-Life**, **Portal**, **Left 4 Dead**, you probably know their stuff

## Honorable Mentions
- [Broken Reality](https://store.steampowered.com/app/757480/Broken_Reality/): a vaporwave old internet adventure game i really liked many years ago
- [Euro Truck Simulator 2](https://store.steampowered.com/app/227300/Euro_Truck_Simulator_2/): buh drive big truck across eu
- [Cities Skylines](https://store.steampowered.com/app/255710/Cities_Skylines/): buh build big city
- [Trove](https://store.steampowered.com/app/304050/Trove/): i used to play trove a LOT as a child, though i wouldn't know whether it's worth trying nowadays
- **Super Paper Mario**: I really loved the darker story of this game as a kid. It's a bit different gameplay-wise than other Paper Mario titles but I love this one regardless
- **Insomniac Spider-Man**: I played Spider-Man 2 for the PS2 as a kid, and that game had really great web swinging mechanics, so I'm really glad that someone finally got it right again over a decade later
- **Persona series**: i enjoy the music and turn based combat in these games a lot
